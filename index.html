George Saint Pierre is a French Canadian born mixed martial artist regarded as one of the best mixed martial artists ever. His highest accomplishments would include being a long reigning champion in the UFC(Ultimate Fighting Championship) in two different divisions before retiring.

These are some different styles of martial arts that GSP has achieved a high level in that are recognized by a belt ranking system. To be a successful mixed martial artist you need to have a high level of multiple styles.

George was also known to be a very skilled wrestler which was rare for somebody training initially in Canada. Usually strong wrestling backgrounds in martial arts are tied to the United States or Russia.
